About Me

As a Visual and Merch Flow Supervisor, I bring a unique combination of creativity and technical expertise to enhance the in-store customer experience. I oversee the visual presentation of products, ensuring that displays are both aesthetically pleasing and strategically placed to drive sales. My role requires a keen eye for detail, an understanding of consumer behavior, and the ability to implement merchandising strategies that align with brand goals.

Before transitioning into this role, I worked in the IT department, where I developed a strong foundation in technology and problem-solving. With a degree in Computer Science, I have the technical skills to analyze data, troubleshoot issues, and implement solutions that improve operational efficiency. This background has allowed me to approach my current position with a systems-oriented mindset, blending both creativity and functionality.

In my current role, I continue to leverage my IT experience to streamline processes, manage digital tools, and ensure that merchandising strategies are executed smoothly. My ability to blend technical knowledge with visual merchandising helps me create impactful, organized, and efficient in-store displays that engage customers and enhance their shopping experience.
