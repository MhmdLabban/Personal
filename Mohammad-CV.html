About Me

I am a retail professional with a strong background in visual merchandising, inventory flow, and a growing expertise in merchandising strategy. Currently working as an Assistant Merchandiser, I support market-wide planning, stock management, and product performance analysis, ensuring alignment between brand direction and in-store execution.

Previously, as a Visual and Merch Flow Supervisor, I specialized in enhancing the customer journey through impactful product presentation and strategic visual displays. My approach combines creativity with operational efficiency, ensuring that merchandising efforts not only attract attention but also drive sales and reflect brand identity.

My career began in the IT field, where I developed a solid foundation in technical problem-solving, systems thinking, and data analysis. With studies in Computer Science and practical experience in IT support, I bring a unique, dual-focused perspective to retail—one that blends technology with visual execution.

By leveraging both creative and technical strengths, I focus on building efficient merchandising solutions, optimizing stock flow, and delivering visually compelling customer experiences. I am passionate about continuous improvement and thrive in dynamic retail environments where adaptability, collaboration, and precision are key.
